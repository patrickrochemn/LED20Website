About Us

We are a group of engineers and computer scientists who love to play DnD together!

This project has continued to grow from initially being a custom dining/game table with a TV built into the table for virtual battle maps and LEDs for ambiance to being a full-scale multi-computer system! The current iteration has a PC built into it for DMs to use during sessions and the LEDs built into the table and surrounding the entire room are individually addressable LEDs (Neopixels) that can be set to various ambient animations, but most importantly enable our custom combat mode. Our custom combat mode puts an LED health bar in front of each player at the table and updates based on changes to their health stat on their player sheet on DnDBeyond.
